While my professional life is driven by systems engineering and grid optimization, my time outside the lab is fueled by movement and exploration. I find that stepping away from complex models to engage with the physical world is essential for maintaining balance and gaining fresh perspectives.

---

### 🧗‍♂️ Bouldering & Climbing
Whether I am solving problems in the lab or on the wall, the process requires focus, adaptability, and persistence. I spend a significant amount of my free time at the bouldering gym, working on new routes and enjoying the physical challenge.

### 🌊 Coastal Road Trips
There is nothing quite like the freedom of a long-distance drive to reset the mind. I frequently pack up the car and head toward the East Coast beaches for a change of scenery.

The best part of these journeys? I am always accompanied by my favorite co-pilots: my black cat, Jiu Jiu, and my dog, Tu Tu.
