Hi, I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Civil and Systems Engineering at [Johns Hopkins University](https://www.jhu.edu/), advised by Professor [Yury Dvorkin](https://engineering.jhu.edu/faculty/uzi-yury-dvorkin/). I am also proudly affiliated with the Ralph O'Connor Sustainable Energy Institute ([ROSEI](https://energyinstitute.jhu.edu/)), the Center for Systems Science and Engineering ([CSSE](https://systems.jhu.edu/)), the Electric Power Innovation for a Carbon-free Society ([EPICS](https://energyinstitute.jhu.edu/epics/)), and the Academic Center for Reliability and Resilience of Offshore Wind ([ARROW](https://arrowosw.org/)). 

My research is highly interdisciplinary and examines the upstream and downstream risks and opportunities within evolving power grids and energy systems. I am particularly focused on system planning, reliability, and resilience as they are threatened and shaped by critical material supply chain constraints, grid-supporting equipment, emerging large-scale loads such as AI data centers, and the policy drivers that guide the energy transition and ensure power supply safety.


Academically, I earned my Master's degree (2022) from the Department of Environmental Health and Engineering at [Johns Hopkins University](https://www.jhu.edu/), advised by Professor [Ben Hobbs](https://engineering.jhu.edu/faculty/benjamin-hobbs/). I received my Bachelor's degree (2020) from the School of Environmental Science and Engineering at [Tianjin University](https://en.tju.edu.cn/). During my senior year, I completed an exchange program and my undergraduate thesis in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the [University of California, Irvine](https://uci.edu/).

Before beginning my Ph.D., I served as a Research Associate (2022–2023) at the [21st Century China Center](https://china.ucsd.edu/) within the School of Global Policy and Strategy at the [University of California, San Diego](https://ucsd.edu/), supervised by Professor [Michael Davidson](https://gps.ucsd.edu/faculty-directory/michael-davidson.html). I also gained valuable industry perspective working as an intern consultant (2021) at [Tsintergy](https://www.tsintergy.com/).
